Open Journals

Open Journals is a collection of open source, open access journals. We currently have four main publications:

  • The Journal of Open Source Software · http://joss.theoj.org
  • The Journal of Open Source Education · http://jose.theoj.org
  • The Open Journal of Astrophysics · http://astro.theoj.org
  • The Journal of Brief Ideas · http://briefideas.org

Open Source

All of our journals run on open source software which is available under our GitHub organization profile: https://github.com/openjournals

Open Access

All of our journals are open access publications with content licensed under a Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International License. Copyright remains with the submitting authors.

Ownership & Governance

Open Journals and each of its journals are volunteer-led, with an editor-in-chief who leads each journal. Open Journals is fiscally sponsored by NumFOCUS, and has a documented governance structure.
